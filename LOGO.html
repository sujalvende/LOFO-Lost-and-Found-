About Us

LOFO (Lost & Found Office System) is a dedicated college platform designed to help students, faculty, and staff report, find, and return lost belongings on campus. It ensures a transparent and organized process to reconnect items with their rightful owners. To maintain honesty and trust, providing false information or falsely claiming ownership of any found item is a serious violation. Anyone found doing so will be penalized with a fine equal to two times the cost of the item, helping keep the system fair and reliable for everyone.

Contact Us

If you have lost an item, found something on campus, or need assistance with the LOFO platform, feel free to reach out to us. You can contact the Lost & Found Office through the provided details or visit the office during working hours. Our team is always ready to help ensure lost belongings are returned safely and promptly. For any queries, reports, or support, we encourage you to connect with us and help keep our campus honest and organized.
